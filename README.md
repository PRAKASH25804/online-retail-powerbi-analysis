# Online Retail Power BI Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes online retail sales data to identify revenue trends,
key markets, and customer insights for business expansion decisions.

## Data Cleaning
- Removed transactions with negative quantities (returns)
- Removed records with negative unit prices
- Created Revenue = Quantity Ã— Unit Price

## Business Questions Answered

### 1. Revenue Trend Over Time (CEO)
A line chart shows monthly revenue trends for 2011, highlighting clear
seasonality and strong year-end growth.

### 2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue (CMO)
A clustered bar chart shows the top revenue-generating countries
(excluding the UK) along with quantities sold.

### 3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue (CMO)
A column chart highlights revenue concentration among key customers,
supporting retention and loyalty strategies.

### 4. Demand by Country (CEO)
A map visualization identifies countries with high demand and strong
expansion potential.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Online Retail Dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)
